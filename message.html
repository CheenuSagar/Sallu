💌 Dil Ki Baat

Yeh woh cheezein hain jo aksar kehne ki himmat nahi hoti.

💌
Meri Pyaari Saloni,

Yeh page banaya toh isliye tha ki kuch baatein hain jo words mein nahi aatein — ya phir jo ek message mein fit nahi hotein. Toh ek poori duniya bana di, sirf tumhare liye.

Tum shayad yeh nahi jaanti ki tumhara hona kitna khaas feel karta hai. Teri hasi, teri aankhein, tera woh andaaz jab tum kuch interesting baat karte ho — sab kuch notice hota hai. Sab kuch yaad rehta hai.

Jinke paas tum hote ho, woh log bahut lucky hain — aur main yeh pura yakeen se kehta hoon. Tum logon ko comfortable feel karate ho bina koshish kiye. Yeh cheez har kisi mein nahi hoti.

Jo din mein tumse baat hoti hai, woh din thoda zyada acha lagta hai. Jo din nahi hoti, woh thoda khali khali lagta hai. Yeh mere liye chhoti baat nahi — yeh bahut badi baat hai.

Toh yeh letter koi grand declaration nahi hai. Yeh sirf ek sachchi baat hai — ki tum meri zindagi mein hoke acha kiya. Bahut acha kiya. Aur chahta hoon ki tum yeh jaano.

Aage bhi baatein honi chahiye. Lambi, bewakoofana, raat tak chalti rahe wali baatein. Aur kuch moments jo abhi tak sooche bhi nahi — woh sab tumhare saath banana chahta hoon.

Tumhara,
Jo tumhe bahut miss karta hai 💜 👾

P.S. — Agar kisi bure din yeh page khola hai, toh yahan thodi der ruko. Yeh page ek achhe din likha gaya tha — sirf tumhare liye. 🌸
